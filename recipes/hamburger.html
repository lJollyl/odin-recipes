Hembober

A decidant slab of meat lovingly embraced by two delicate chunks of grain, with an endless combination of toppings and condiments to go with.

Ingredients

Required

  • One cow meat slab.
  • Two chunks or thin slabs of grain.

Optional popular ingredients for added decidance

  • pickle slices.
  • Tomato blood.
  • mustard.
  • Mayonaisse.
  • Cheese.

Directions

  1. Grill your meat slab
  2. Place grilled meat slab onto one of your chunks or slices of grain.
  3. Add your optional ingredients on top of the meat slab.
  4. Cover your meat slab with your other chunk or slice of grain.
