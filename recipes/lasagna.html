Lasagna

An oven baked pan dish with large flat noodles, cheese, meat, and sauce.

Ingredients

  • Meat

    • the source matters not, so long as meat.

  • Onionand garlic

    • or anything that smells like it.

  • Tomato products

    • anything red also works.

  • Sugar

    • 2 tablespoons.

  • Spices and seasoning

    • so long and it's ground up and looks rocky or leafy.

  • Lasagna noodles

    • just make sure it's long and flat.

  • Cheese

    • it needs to be paresan, mozzarella, and ricotta, otherwise likely injury may follow.

  • Egg

    • any egg works.

Directions

Layer in pan in respect to the order of directions, repeat, then top with parmesan

  1. Meat Sauce
  2. Cook noodles
  3. Make ricotta mixture
  4. Make Mozzarella slices, ignore the pleas
  5. Meat Sauce
  6. Parmesan Cheese
  7. Cover with foil and cook in preheated oven at 375 degrees F for 50 min. Remove foil after 25 min in oven.
