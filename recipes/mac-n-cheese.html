Mac n Cheese

Cheesy noodle dish, perfect for an easy mid-day meal.

Ingredients

  • Shell or elbow noodles.
  • A cheese of your choosing.
  • Butter.
  • Milk.

Directions

  1. Boil noodles in a pan of water for 7-9 minutes.
  2. Melt your cheese either on stovetop, in the oven, or in a heat-safe bowl in the microwave.
  3. Strain waters from noodles, do not rinse the noodles.
  4. Add a spoonful of butter.
  5. Pour in a bit of milk.
  6. Pour in your cheese and stir until noodles are consistently cheesy.
